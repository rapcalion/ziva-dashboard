Project Overview

Project Goal

To provide a comprehensive, integrated digital marketing solution for Ziva Jewellery. The primary objective is to elevate Ziva's online brand presence, enhance digital customer acquisition, and build sustained engagement across all relevant digital touchpoints to support the brand's digital commerce objectives.
